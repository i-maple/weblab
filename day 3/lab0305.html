This is a picture of a Cessna 210. The 210 is the flagship single-engine Cessna aircraft. Although the 210 began as a four-place
aircraft, it soon acquired a third row of sears, stretching it to a sic-place plane.The 210 is classified as a high-preformance airplane,
which means its landing gear is retractable and its engine has more than 200 horsepower. In its first model year, which was 1960,
the 210 was powered by a 260-horsepower fuel-injected six-cylinder engine that displaced 471 cubic inches. The 210 is the fastest
singel-engine airplane ever built by Cessna.
